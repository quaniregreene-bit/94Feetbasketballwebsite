About 94 Feet

More than just a brand — 94 Feet is a mindset. We’re here to shape the next generation of athletes, leaders, and dreamers.

Our Mission

At 94 Feet, our mission is to help athletes reach their full potential on and off the court. We’re dedicated to creating a culture of excellence, discipline, and confidence — starting with training and extending to mentorship, community, and competition.

Why "94 Feet"?

The name "94 Feet" represents the full length of a basketball court — and the effort, hustle, and heart it takes to play the game the right way. We carry that same energy into everything we do.

What We Offer

  • Professional-level training for all ages and skill levels
  • Leagues and events that push players to compete and grow
  • Mentorship programs that build character, not just skills
  • Quality athletic wear that represents the grind

Community Driven

94 Feet is built by hoopers, for hoopers. We’re proud to serve our local communities by providing opportunities to grow through sport, teamwork, and education.
